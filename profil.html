Company Overview

About KMDS

Koura Mining and Drilling Services (KMDS) was established in 2023 in Mali and in 2024 in Côte d’Ivoire by Ivorian founders. We are specialize in Air Core, Grade Control, Diamond Drilling and Reverse Circulation drilling for exploration and mining companies focused on gold, copper, lithium, and base metals.

Our Mission Vision and Values

Our goal is to become the leading local drilling company.
